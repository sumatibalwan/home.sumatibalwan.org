Tribute to Dr. M.N. Hardikar

Honoring his legacy and dedication

Legacy of Compassion

Dr. M.N. Hardikar generously donated one acre of land to establish a charitable trust in honor of his late mother, Radhabai. The trust, named the Radhabai Hardikar Pranijat Mangal Sanstha, continues to foster a spirit of compassion and community service.
