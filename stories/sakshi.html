Sakshi Shinde

In 2018, 10-year-old Sakshi Shinde was rescued from a life of hardship, having been abandoned by her family and left to suffer under the care of her alcoholic father. When she arrived at Pakhrmaya, an orphanage dedicated to giving hope to children without families, she carried the weight of a painful past. Though academics were a challenge for her, Sakshi discovered a fierce passion for sports, especially karate and kabaddi, where her determination and resilience quickly set her apart. Despite her struggles, Sakshi's dedication led her to excel in sports, winning gold at the state level and bronze at the national level in karate. Now in the 9th standard, she continues to thrive as a talented athlete, embodying the spirit of hope and perseverance.
