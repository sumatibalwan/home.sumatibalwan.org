A Journey from Rescue to Independence

Shweta Indulikar quickly adapted to her new environment, and her talents soon began to shine. She completed her schooling at Sumati Balwan, excelling academically up to the 10th grade. Driven by a passion for understanding the human mind, she pursued and graduated with a degree in Psychology. But Shweta's journey went beyond academics. She had a deep love for sports, particularly Karate and football. Her dedication to Karate led her to earn gold medals in state, district, and national competitions, eventually representing her country on the international stage. Her adventurous spirit also saw her complete commando training, showcasing her physical and mental strength. Aspiring to serve her country, she began applying for positions in the defense sector while also working as a gym trainer, sharing her passion for fitness. Today, Shweta Indulikar stands as a symbol of perseverance and success. Her transformation from a young girl from a small village to a strong, independent woman with numerous achievements is an inspiration to all at Pakharmaya. Her story is a testament to resilience, determination, and the power of a supportive community
